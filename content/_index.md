This whole blogging racket feels like a sprawling digital carnival: too many loud opinions, too much polished certainty, and a suspicious number of people pretending they know exactly what they’re doing.

So here is `semog.nl`, stumbling into the noise with no sacred roadmap and no respectable content strategy. I have no real idea what will end up here. Maybe notes on software, systems, and whatever else rattles loose. Maybe dispatches from the edge of building things. Maybe nothing at all. For all I know, this becomes a blog about nothing. Bear with me.
