This whole blogging racket feels like a sprawling digital carnival: too many loud opinions, too much polished certainty, and a suspicious number of people pretending they know exactly what they’re doing.

So here is `semog.nl`, wobbling into the mess with no grand manifesto, no sacred content strategy, and not the faintest guarantee of consistency. Maybe this becomes a place for notes on software, systems, and whatever else crawls out of the machinery. Maybe it turns into a blog about nothing. Maybe I post once and vanish in a puff of static. Bear with me.
